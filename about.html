Our Vision

Our vision is to conduct high-quality multidisciplinary research on population composition, demographic change and health policies and programmes in India.We aim to be recognized as premier organization for monitoring and evaluation of National Health Mission (NHM) of India to provide insights for program implementation and course-corrections at district, state and national level.The overall vision is to be recognized as a dynamic organization and a center of excellence on population health policies and programmes in India.
